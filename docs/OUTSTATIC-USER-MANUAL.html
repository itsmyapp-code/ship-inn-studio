2. Understanding the Dashboard

The left sidebar has three sections:

  • Pages — Controls the core website pages (Home, Food & Drink, Contact, etc.).
  • Events — Controls upcoming events (live music, specials, etc.).
  • News — Controls news articles.
  • Gallery — Controls the photos shown on the Gallery page.
  • Media Library — Where you upload all images.

3. How to Upload and Use Images

Step 1: Upload the image

  1. In the left sidebar, click Media Library.
  2. Click Add Media and select your image from your computer.
  3. The image will appear in the library.

Step 2: Use the image

For standard pages (Cover Images):

  1. Go to the page you want to edit.
  2. Click the Cover Image field in the right sidebar.
  3. It will open an image picker — select your image and click Save.

For the Hero Images (Home Page only):

  1. Right-click your uploaded image in the Media Library.
  2. Choose "Open image in new tab".
  3. Look at the browser address bar — your image path is /images/ followed by the filename (e.g., /images/ship-inn.png).
  4. Go to Pages → Home and paste that path into the Hero Image text fields.
Note: Image picker fields have a visual preview. You'll see a thumbnail of the selected image, with a "Remove" button to clear it.

4. How to Edit and Save Any Page

  1. In the left sidebar, click Pages, then click the page you want to edit.
  2. The editor opens. Ignore the large centre text editor for Pages. It is not used on the live website.
  3. Scroll down the right sidebar to find all the important fields that control the live website layout.
  4. If a field shows + Create, click it to open the input box, then type your value.
  5. Ensure Status says "Published" (not Draft).
  6. Click the black Save button (top-right).
  7. Wait 1–2 minutes, then refresh the live website to see your changes.

6. How to Manage the Gallery in Detail

The Gallery page is entirely dynamic and controlled by the Gallery Collection in the sidebar. This makes it incredibly easy to add or remove photos without touching any code.

To Add a New Photo:

  1. Click Gallery under Collections in the left sidebar.
  2. Click the black "New" button in the top right corner.
  3. Title: Type a descriptive name for the photo (e.g., "Roast Dinner" or "Front Bar"). This is just for your reference in the dashboard.
  4. Description: (Optional) You can add a short caption here if you want it to display on the photo.
  5. Cover Image: Click the large grey box that says "Click to add an image". This opens your Media Library. Select the photo you want to display.
  6. Category: In the right sidebar, find the Category dropdown. You must select a category (Food & Drink, Exterior, Interior, Rooms, or Surroundings). This perfectly maps to the buttons at the top of your live gallery page, allowing users to filter by that category.
  7. Ensure the Status dropdown is set to "Published".
  8. Click the Save button. The live website will rebuild and the photo will appear in the gallery within 60 seconds!

To Edit or Delete an Existing Photo:

  1. Click Gallery in the left sidebar to view the list of all uploaded gallery photos.
  2. To change a category: Click the photo in the list, change the Category dropdown in the right sidebar, and click Save.
  3. To remove a photo completely: Hover over the photo in the list and click the trash can icon (🗑️) on the far right. Confirm the deletion, and it will be permanently removed from the live gallery.

7. How to Add a New Event or News Article

  1. Click Events or News in the left sidebar.
  2. Click the "New" button (top right).
  3. In the centre screen: Type your headline in the Title box, then write the full article below it.
  4. In the right sidebar: Pick the Date, add a Cover Image, and add a short Description.
  5. Change Status to "Published" and click Save.
